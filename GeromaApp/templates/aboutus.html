ABOUT SICA MUBS

SICA is Students Information & Computing Association the MUBS ICT Association that officially came into existence in Makerere University Business School on 7th September 2018 with a launch with various administrators from MUBS. It is initially a brain child of a group of Computing students under the faculty of Computing and Informatics who realised thee need not only to have a binding academic Association but also for it to act as a platform to impact students positively through equipping them with practical ICT skills and knowledge both given in and out of lectures of the different MUBS programs.

MISION

"To impact high quality and Advanced Technical Education and make a positive difference in students to become leaders in the I.T.Industry.”

VISION

“To Enhance Business with Technology, create the Best Personal Computing Experience to Students ,and to breed creative Professionals through Innovative Business I.T.Strategies.”

MOTTO

Empower Innovate & Motivate.
