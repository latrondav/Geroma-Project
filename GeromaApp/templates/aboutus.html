About Geroma Logistics Limited

Geroma Logistics Limited is a specialized logistics partner with expertise in customs clearance, efficient freight forwarding solutions, and expert tax consultation services. With over 15 years of experience, we have built a strong reputation for dedication to client satisfaction and compliance with customs and tax regulations. Our mission is to empower international trade, innovate logistics solutions, and motivate success for our valued clients.

Customs Clearance

Our customs clearance services ensure smooth and hassle-free clearance of goods through customs procedures, ensuring compliance with all regulations.

Freight Forwarding

We offer efficient freight forwarding solutions, ensuring timely and secure delivery of goods to their destinations.

Tax Consultation

Our expert tax consultation services provide valuable guidance and strategies to optimize tax-related matters for our clients.
